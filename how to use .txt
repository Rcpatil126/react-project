Step 1: User needs to install npm on his/her device 

Step 2: User has to start npm

Step 3: They need to open the browser and then open localhost:3000

Step 4: After opening the localhost:3000 the user can see the Registration form.

Step 4: The user is required to fill the Registration form.

Step 5: After completely filling the form, click on the registration button and submit the information

Step 6: After clicking on the registration button you will be directed to a different page where you can see the information which you have filled in the form. 

Step 7: On clicking back button you will be back on the registration form.
